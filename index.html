Almond Tofu dish

Table of Contents

  • Steps for making almond tofu
  • Images of almond tofu
Ingredients
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp almond extract
  • 1 tbsp gelatin
  • 1/2 cup cold water
Steps
  1. Mix gelatin with 1/2 cup cold water
  2. Heat 1 and 1/2 cups of milk and add sugar
  3. Mix in the gelatin
  4. Add in heavy cream
  5. Let it cool to room temperature
  6. Refrigerate for at least 6 hours
  7. Done!
