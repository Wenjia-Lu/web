Almond Tofu Dish

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp almond extract
  • 1 tbsp gelatin
  • 1/2 cup cold water

Steps

  1. Mix gelatin with cold water
  2. Heat milk and add sugar
  3. Mix gelatin into sugar-milk mixture
  4. Add in heavy cream
  5. Cool to room temperature
  6. Refrigerate for at least 6 hours
  7. Done!
